Front end:
    Single page app with product listing with stock and a buy button. 
    Right side drawer for uploading files.
    Redux for state management (redux-thunk and react-redux).

Backend:
    Node JS express application. 
    Mongo DB as database. 
    Mongoose package for Mongo DB schema management.
    One route for uploading products.json and one route for inventory.json
    One route for returning products with their calcualted stock.
    One route for selling a product and reducing stock. 
    JWT token (optional).
